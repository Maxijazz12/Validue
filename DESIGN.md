# VLDTA Design System

Use `globals.css` as the source of truth for actual tokens and values. Prefer Tailwind token classes over raw values.

## Visual Direction

- Clean, modern, slightly editorial
- Soft neutral surfaces with warm accent energy
- Light motion, subtle depth, not flashy
- Rounded but not bubbly
- Trustworthy, premium, and calm

## Defaults

- DM Sans primary, Instrument Serif for selective display moments
- Light surfaces, dark charcoal dark mode
- Soft borders, subtle shadows, warm hover glow
- Gentle fade/slide entrances and restrained micro-interactions

## Design-Critical Areas

- landing hero
- pricing
- wall card
- draft review flow
- `globals.css`

When changing product-facing UI, preserve clarity, trust, and perceived signal quality over decorative novelty.
