# Product Intelligence

## Evidence Hierarchy

1. Real user behavior
2. Explicit user/founder feedback
3. External product patterns
4. Simulated reactions
5. Internal reasoning

Higher-tier evidence overrides lower-tier evidence.

## Simulation Rule

Use simulation to generate objections, confusion points, pricing reactions, and better tests.
Never use simulation as proof.

## External Example Rule

Extract the principle, not the surface.
Ask why it works for them and whether that transfers here.
Treat outside examples as hypotheses until tested in VLDTA's context.

## Anti-Bullshit Rules

- Simulated behavior is not proof
- One external example is not causal truth
- Tier 4–5 evidence creates hypotheses, not best practices
- Capture conditions, not just tactics
- Label evidence tier on every lesson
- Keep lessons few and high signal
